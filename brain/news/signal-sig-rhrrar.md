**Why:** Directly maps to our thesis that task-level efficiency doesn't move the P&L — and our ICP is the CFO under exactly this board pressure.

45% of finance leaders focus AI on productivity; only 20% prioritize decision quality, and just 22% have linked AI spend to business outcomes despite board pressure.
